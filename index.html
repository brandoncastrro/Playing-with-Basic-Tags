What's a World Cup?

Soccer, or how some of us call it "The Beautiful Game" has the most important tournament every 4 years. Bringing the best national teams to fight for one of the most important trophies in sports. Before a World Cup starts, these national teams need to work in ranking up and win a couple of tournament to make it to the list of qualified teams. This event is important for many people because it represent their countries. Who wouldn't want that cup to go home, Right? Without further do, let me share some facts with you:

  • Uruguay were the hosts of the first World Cup played back in 1930, they also won that year.
  • In 2014, around 3.2 billion people were watching the World Cup.
  • Brazil has won this tournament five times, making them the nation with most cup in their hands.
  • In 1966, the World Cup was stolen and couldn't be found for 7 days until a dog named Pickles found it.
  • Brazil and Italy are the only nations to have won this tournament twice in a row.
